\section*{Eigenvalues and Diagonalization}

\exercise{Characteristic polynomial}
Find the characteristic polynomial of $A=\begin{pmatrix}2&1\\1&2\end{pmatrix}$ and its eigenvalues.

\exercise{Orthogonality}
Show that eigenvectors of a real symmetric matrix corresponding to distinct eigenvalues are orthogonal.
