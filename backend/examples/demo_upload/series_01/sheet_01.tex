\section*{Vectors and Matrices}

\exercise{Matrix warm-up}
Given $A=\begin{pmatrix}1&2\\3&4\end{pmatrix}$ and $B=\begin{pmatrix}0&1\\1&0\end{pmatrix}$,
compute $A+B$ and $A\,B$.

\exercise{Dot products}
Let $v=(1,2,3)$ and $w=(2,0,-1)$. Compute $v\cdot w$ and $\lVert v\rVert$.
